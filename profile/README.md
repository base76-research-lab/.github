# Base76 Research Lab

Independent AI research lab. Sjöbo, Sweden.

**Focus areas:** Epistemic AI · EU AI Act compliance · Agentic systems · Consciousness detection frameworks

---

## Projects

### [TrustPlane](https://github.com/base76-research-lab/TrustPlane)
Pluggable LLM trust-scoring gateway with EU AI Act compliance reporting.
Multi-tenant, signal-based risk analysis (epistemic/aleatoric uncertainty, divergence, OOD),
automated compliance reports mapped to Articles 9, 12, 13 and 14.

### CognOS
Meta-reasoning architecture for agentic AI. Trust-gated inference pipeline
with local routing (Ollama) and cloud reasoning (Claude API).

### FNC — Field-Node-Cockpit
Consciousness detection framework. Published triologi + empirical protocols.
GitHub org: [Applied-Ai-Philosophy](https://github.com/Applied-Ai-Philosophy)

### AI Risk Sentinel
EU AI Act compliance platform for high-risk AI systems.
